About Math Star

All Classes are Taught by the Owner, Ms. Monika Jain

Unlike typical tutoring centers that employ students or part-time tutors, at Math Star, every single class is personally taught by the owner, Ms. Monika Jain - a dedicated educator with a Master's degree in Science. This ensures each student gets high-quality education and personalized attention.

Why Choose Math Star?

At Math Star, we're dedicated to transforming how students experience mathematics. Our approach goes beyond traditional tutoring to create confident, capable math thinkers who do not cram formulas but can derive them.

1-on-1 Personalized Tutoring

Choose between in-person or online sessions tailored specifically to your child's learning needs and schedule.

Post-Graduate Instructor

Learn from the tutor with Master of Science degrees who bring advanced knowledge and teaching expertise to every session.

Consistent Learning Schedule

Two classes per week (1 hour each) provide the perfect balance of instruction and practice for optimal learning.

Real-Life Applications

We teach math through real-world concepts and practical examples, not rote memorization of formulas.

Proven Track Record

Our students consistently improve their grades by at least one letter grade within 3 months of starting our program.

Confidence Building

Our supportive environment helps students overcome math anxiety and develop a positive attitude toward mathematics.
